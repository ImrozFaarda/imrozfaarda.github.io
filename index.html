Welcome

I am a PhD Candidate at the University of Amsterdam (UvA) in the Department of Geography, Planning and International Development Studies (GPIO). My research explores the dynamic landscapes of socio-technical transitions, focusing on the influence of narratives in bottom-up mobility innovations.

With a background spanning mechanical engineering, ecology, and sustainable development from the Tata Institute of Social Sciences (TISS), my work is deeply rooted in organizing for societal grand challenges in Global South contexts, spatial planning, and inclusive development.
